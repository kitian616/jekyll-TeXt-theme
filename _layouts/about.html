Introduction

Collaboration, decentralization and digital democracy: three keys to combat climate change. Welcome to the Decentralized Climate Foundation.

About Us

The Decentralized Climate Foundation (DCF) is a non-profit organization based in Mexico dedicated to supporting DECA and other decentralized environmental and social projects focused on combating climate change.


Mission

The mission of the Decentralized Climate Foundation is to increase the participation of individuals in climate change actions. The Foundation allow individuals to participate in the voluntary carbon market & support the creation of a decentralized person-to-person carbon credit market which will be converted in the future into a decentralized climate fund.
